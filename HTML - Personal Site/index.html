Well.. I love books, so I thought it was a good idea to indicate some of them and comment a little.

Sooo....Let's get started!

The red queen

The book "The Red Queen" is one of the best I have ever read.
It is about a kingdom that is divided by two types of blood:
silver (a blood that has powers) and red (a "normal" blood).
One day, a red-blooded girl called "Mare", enters the royal castle like a cleaning lady,
in order to overthrow the silver ones from power, but discovers that she had secret powers,
letting several problems get in the way of her true objective...

Queen of Hearts

This book is about the Queen of Hearts' point of view of her past
and the misfortunes that have occurred for her to change completely.

the selection

"The selection" was the best book I have ever read!
He tells the story about a bunch of girls who are selected to try to win the prince's heart.
This story also contains a forbidden love,
an inevitable love
and a choice of love.

Twilight
